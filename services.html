Services

For as little as $17 per visit, we will check the perimeter of your home looking for damage, unusual conditions or breach of home security. We will gather any mail, deliveries or papers and place them inside your home. While inside we will check that your A/C is working and your electricity is on. Our most popular Plan 2 includes all water services. We will turn on the water and flush/clean all drains and toilets. Upon departure, we will turn off the water and secure your home. We're done and you have the peace of mind knowing your home is taken care of while you are away.

Rental Management Services

If you are one of our clients who occasionally or seasonally rents your home and does so by utilizing one of the “Rental by Owner” sites, Palmer Ranch HomeWatchers® has a management plan for you:

From afar, you do the rental schedule, make the rental agreements and collect the money and deposits. Locally, we greet the renters, supply keys and are available for any problems during the stay. We do the check out and take inventory of the home and supplies. We then have the home cleaned and fix anything that needs repair and prepare for the next renters or owner occupancy.
